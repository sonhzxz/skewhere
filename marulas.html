MANG ROMY'S LUGAW

In the heart of Marulas, Valenzuela City, a bowl of warm and comforting goodness awaits you at Mang Romy's Lugaw. This humble street food stall has been serving up a beloved Filipino classic for years, drawing locals and visitors alike with its delicious and soul-satisfying lugaw. Join us as we delve into the world of Mang Romy's Lugaw and discover why it has become a go-to spot for a hearty and comforting meal.

Mang Romy's Lugaw in Marulas, Valenzuela City, is a haven for those seeking a hearty and comforting meal. With their perfectly cooked rice porridge, flavorful toppings, and an inviting atmosphere, this humble street food stall has captured the hearts and palates of locals and visitors alike. So, if you find yourself in Valenzuela City, take a moment to indulge in the comforting embrace of Mang Romy's Lugaw. It's an experience that will leave you feeling nourished, satisfied, and connected to the rich culinary traditions of the Philippines.
