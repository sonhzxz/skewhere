SM SEASIDE STREET FOOD

As the Christmas spirit fills the air, SM MOA Seaside Street Food comes alive with a festive ambiance that captivates both locals and tourists alike. This culinary fiesta offers a tantalizing array of flavors, showcasing the best of Filipino street food traditions infused with a touch of holiday cheer. From savory delights to sweet treats, every bite is a celebration of the season.

No Christmas celebration is complete without indulging in sweet delights, and SM MOA Seaside Street Food delivers an array of treats to satisfy your cravings. Treat yourself to the nostalgic comfort of "Bibingka," a warm rice cake cooked in clay pots and topped with butter, grated coconut, and salted egg. The aroma of freshly baked Bibingka fills the air, enticing passersby to savor this traditional Christmas treat.

SM MOA Seaside Street Food is not just a culinary experience; it's a celebration of the Christmas season. The lively atmosphere is adorned with twinkling lights, festive decorations, and joyful music, creating a magical ambiance that adds to the dining experience. Explore the stalls, engage in conversations with the friendly vendors, and let the festive vibes envelop you.
